Introduction

Welcome to Trending Kenyan Celebrities. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app.

Important: Our app does not collect, store, or process any personal information from visitors. We believe in complete privacy for our users.

This Privacy Policy applies to Trending Kenyan Celebrities and explains our no-data-collection approach.

Information We Do NOT Collect

To be completely transparent, here is what we do NOT collect when you visit our app:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any other tracking technologies.
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics services that track user behavior.
  • No Server Logs: Our server is configured to not store IP addresses or any other connection data.
  • No Third-Party Data Sharing: Since we don't collect data, we have nothing to share with third parties.

How Our app Works

Trending Kenyan Celebrities is a static app that provides information about popular Kenyan celebrities. The content is updated daily but does not require any user interaction that would necessitate data collection.

You can browse our app anonymously without leaving any digital footprint.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services that might collect data on our behalf.

If our app contains links to other sites, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any app you visit through our links.

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any data, we do not have specific concerns about children's privacy.

Your Privacy Rights

Since we don't collect any data, you don't need to worry about:

  • Requesting access to your data
  • Asking us to correct your data
  • Requesting deletion of your data
  • Objecting to our use of your data
  • Asking for data portability

All these rights are automatically respected because we have no data about you.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
